# Automate Your User Insights: Introducing the Hotjar Node for n8n

We're excited to announce the release of our new Hotjar community node for n8n! At Velocity BPA, we're committed to building automation solutions that bridge the gaps in your workflow, and this node does exactly that for teams relying on Hotjar for user experience insights.

## The Problem: Manual Data Wrangling

If you're using Hotjar to understand user behavior, you know how valuable survey responses, session recordings, and heatmap data can be. But manually exporting this data, syncing it with your CRM, updating stakeholder reports, or managing GDPR deletion requests creates bottlenecks that slow down your team and delay actionable insights.

## The Solution: Seamless Hotjar Automation

Our n8n-nodes-hotjar brings Hotjar's powerful analytics directly into your n8n workflows. Now you can automatically pull survey responses into your database, trigger notifications when specific feedback is received, sync user insights to your product management tools, or handle GDPR deletion requests programmatically.

## Key Features

The Hotjar node supports the operations that matter most:

- **Survey Responses**: Automatically retrieve and process user feedback from your Hotjar surveys
- **Session Recordings**: Access session recording data to integrate with your analysis pipeline
- **Heatmaps**: Pull heatmap data for automated reporting and analysis
- **GDPR Deletion**: Handle data deletion requests compliantly and automatically

Whether you're a product team automating user research workflows, a marketing team syncing feedback to your attribution tools, or a compliance team managing data requests, this node has you covered.

## Getting Started

Installation is simple. In your n8n instance, navigate to Settings > Community Nodes and search for "hotjar", or install via npm:


npm install n8n-nodes-hotjar


Once installed, you'll find the Hotjar node in your n8n node palette, ready to authenticate and connect to your Hotjar account.
